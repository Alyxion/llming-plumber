About Us

Golden Crust Bakery started as a family dream in a tiny kitchen in 1998. Today we still use the same recipes passed down through three generations. Every loaf is shaped by hand, every pastry made from scratch before dawn. We source our flour from local mills and our eggs from free-range farms down the road. No shortcuts, no preservatives — just honest baking.
